## 📖 About the Project

**MuteMate** is a Class of 2026 graduation project developed by students of the **BIDT (Business Information & Digital Transformation)** program at **Helwan National University**. 

In Egypt, many members of the deaf and mute community face daily challenges in expressing themselves and interacting with society. MuteMate seeks to bridge this gap by offering innovative digital tools that ensure their voices are heard, respected, and understood.

---

## 🎯 Vision & Mission

> **Our Vision:** To build a fully inclusive Egyptian society where everyone, regardless of physical ability, has equal access to communication, education, and professional opportunities.

> **Our Mission:** To develop a scalable, user-friendly mobile application that provides real-time translation and communication aids tailored specifically for the Egyptian deaf and mute community.

---

## ✨ Key Features (Planned)

* 🤟 **Sign-to-Text & Text-to-Sign:** Real-time translation to bridge the gap between sign language users and non-signers.
* 🎙️ **Speech-to-Text (Transcribe):** Instantly transcribes spoken words into readable text.
* 🆘 **Emergency Mode:** Quick-access phrases and location sharing for urgent situations.
* 📚 **Learning Hub:** Resources for the general public to learn basic Egyptian Sign Language (ESL).
